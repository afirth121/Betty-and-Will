All About Pigs: How They Look

Learn about the unique features of pigs!

General Characteristics

Pigs are stout, short animals with thick bodies and small legs. They come in various sizes depending on the breed, with farm pigs being larger and smaller breeds like pot-bellied pigs being compact.

Skin and Color

Pigs have skin that can vary in color, ranging from pink, white, black, and brown to spotted patterns. Their skin is often smooth, but some breeds can have wrinkled skin.

Snout and Ears

The pig's snout is one of its most iconic features, perfect for rooting in the ground. Their large, floppy ears can move depending on their mood or surroundings.

Curly Tails

Pigs are known for their curly tails, which are another unique characteristic. These tails help them maintain balance and are often a sign of a happy, healthy pig.

Hooves

Pigs have cloven hooves, which means their hooves are split into two parts. This helps them balance and move easily across various terrains.
